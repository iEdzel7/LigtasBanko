    Phishing Information

    Phishing
    Phishing is when someone pretends to be someone they're not, like a friend or a company, to trick you into giving them your personal information, like your password or bank details. They might send you an email or a message that looks real, but it's actually a trick to steal your information. So, it's important to be careful and not share your private information with anyone you don't know or trust.
    Learn More
    Benign
    Benign means something is safe or harmless. In the context of the internet, a benign email, link, or website is one that doesn't try to trick you or harm your computer or personal information. It's like when you get a friendly message from a friend or visit a website that just has fun games or interesting information, and it doesn't cause any problems for you.
