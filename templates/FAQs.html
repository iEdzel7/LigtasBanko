    What is the primary objective of the LigtasBanko system?
    The system aims to detect phishing websites targeting Philippine banks using deep learning models and auxiliary tools to improve cybersecurity and user awareness.
    How does the Email PwN Checker enhance security?
    It alerts users if their email accounts have been compromised in a data breach, allowing them to take preventive measures.
    What role does the Chatbot play in the system?
    The Chatbot provides real-time assistance, explains why a URL is classified as phishing or benign, and educates users about phishing threats.
    How does the Website Blocker contribute to user protection?
    It restricts user access to identified phishing websites, preventing potential phishing attacks.
    Why is phishing a critical issue in the Philippines?
    The country has high internet usage but low digital literacy, making users more susceptible to phishing attacks, which account for significant financial losses.
    What potential does LigtasBanko have for broader applications?
    The system's scalable design allows for future enhancements to address emerging phishing techniques and broader cybersecurity challenges.
